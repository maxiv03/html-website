Maximiliano Vega

I'm a Junior Front-End Developer and a Glitch Art artist. I have a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I enjoy creating captivating user interfaces that bring designs to life. Collaborating with designers to implement their vision and ensuring a seamless user experience is something I find both challenging and rewarding.

Aside from my programming skills, I'm also passionate about Glitch Art. I love exploring the digital realm and transforming images into visually striking and unconventional pieces. Embracing digital imperfections and pushing the boundaries of traditional art is where my creativity shines.

Combining my expertise in front-end development and glitch art, I bring a unique perspective to my work. I approach web design with an artistic eye and a penchant for experimentation. My goal is to create innovative and visually stunning digital experiences that leave a lasting impact on users.
